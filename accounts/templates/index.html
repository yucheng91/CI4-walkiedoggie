Singapore’s Volunteer-Run Dog Shelter


SOSD is a volunteer-run organisation dedicated to the welfare of Singapore’s many street dogs. We vehemently oppose the culling of stray animals and believe that there is a more humane way to manage this problem. Our dog shelter exists to rescue, rehabilitate and rehome strays to give them a second chance at life. If you’re an animal lover with a great passion for our canine friends, there are plenty of ways you can participate and help give these beautiful creatures a chance to find their forever home. Whether you want to adopt a dog, be a dog shelter volunteer, donate to our pet shelter, become a sponsor or even a foster parent while our dogs await their forever homes, there are plenty of ways to show you care.

Volunteer at our animal shelter by walking with the shelter dog!


If you don’t have the space or time it takes to care for an animal, there are still ways that you can help. As we are a not-for-profit organisation, we are constantly short of resources and our kennel is stretched to the limit. Choosing to donate to our pet shelter is one way you can help; you can donate money or items and we even have a wish list of things that we need to keep the shelter running on a continual basis. Another option is sponsorship, you can choose a dog to sponsor on a month-to-month basis (min 3 months), which helps to cover the expenses incurred, until they find their forever home.
